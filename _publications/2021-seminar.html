Robustness in a sense, that perturbing the input does not lead to a false prediction of the model. The presented paper provides a conceptual introduction to adversarial training as well as a review of current methods. While it is not possible to discuss all adversarial training approaches, some recent advancements are described in detail and their advantages and disadvantages are highlighted. Additionally, pos- sible directions for future research are presented.
